Naming Conventions for Raw Bank Dumps

 - In general:

     isp-board-mnemonic_version-mask-bank_dump.xz

   Examples:

     myrepublic-vbnt-l_16.3.7190-2761005-bank_dump.xz
     comhem-gant-1_15.53.6568-1721005-bank_dump.xz

 - Otherwise, when known RBIs stick to a rigid custover convention, use the same one.

   Examples:

     iiNet:	vbnt-1_18.3.0157-ver3.2.1-CRF905-bank_dump.xz
     Telstra:	vbnt-j_CRF640-16.3.7413-660-RF-bank_dump.xz
